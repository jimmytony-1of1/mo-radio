About Us

The Voice of the Coast

MO RADIO 88.2 FM is Mombasa's most urban radio station — bringing you the pulse of the coast through music, news, talk, and culture.

Broadcasting live from the heart of Mombasa, we reach listeners across the Kenyan Coast and beyond. From the latest hits to deep-rooted Swahili culture, MO RADIO is your home frequency.

We are more than a radio station — we are a community. Our presenters, producers, and partners share one mission: to keep the coast informed, entertained, and connected.
